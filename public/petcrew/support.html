How do I add a pet?

On the home screen, tap the "Add Pet" button. Fill in your pet's name, type, feeding schedule, and optionally add a photo. Your pet will appear on the home screen immediately.

How do I log a quick feeding?

Tap the "Quick Feed" button on any pet card. The feeding is logged instantly with the current time. You can also use the Quick Feed button on your iOS home screen widget!
