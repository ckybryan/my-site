Privacy Policy

Effective Date: October 22, 2025 | Last Updated: October 22, 2025

TL;DR:

  • Free users (no account): Your data stays on your device only. We collect nothing.
  • Premium users (household sync): We securely store your pet data in the cloud to enable real-time sync across devices. We never sell your data.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Free Users (No Account Required)

If you use PetCrew without creating a household (free tier), we collect:

  • ZERO personal information
  • ZERO cloud-stored data
  • All pet feeding logs, schedules, and preferences are stored locally on your device only using secure on-device storage (MMKV)

We do collect anonymous analytics to improve the app:

  • App crashes and errors (via crash reporting)
  • Basic usage statistics (e.g., "user logged a feeding" - no pet names or personal details)
  • Device type and iOS version (for compatibility testing)

What we DON'T collect from free users:

  • ❌ No email address
  • ❌ No phone number
  • ❌ No name or personal identifiers
  • ❌ No pet data (stays on your device)
  • ❌ No location data
  • ❌ No payment information

1.2 Premium Users (Household Sync Enabled)

If you upgrade to Premium and create/join a household, we collect:

Account Information:

  • Anonymous user ID (auto-generated, not linked to your identity)
  • Email address (only if you choose to add it for account recovery - optional)
  • Household invitation codes (for inviting family members)

Pet Data (stored in secure cloud database):

  • Pet names, types, breeds
  • Feeding schedules and logs (timestamps, portion sizes, notes)
  • Feeding history and statistics
  • Member names (e.g., "Mom", "Dad", "John") for tracking who fed the pet

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 Free Users

  • Crash reporting: To fix bugs and improve app stability
  • Anonymous analytics: To understand which features are used most
  • App performance: To optimize speed and reliability

2.2 Premium Users

  • Household sync: To sync your pet data across all devices in your household in real-time
  • Family coordination: To show which family member fed the pet and when
  • Push notifications: To send feeding reminders (only if you enable notifications)
  • Account management: To manage your Premium subscription
  • Customer support: To help you if you contact us with issues

We will NEVER:

  • ❌ Sell your data to third parties
  • ❌ Use your pet data for advertising
  • ❌ Share your information with anyone outside your household (except as required by law)
  • ❌ Track your location
  • ❌ Access your photos, contacts, or other personal data without permission

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Free Users (Local Storage Only)

  • All data stored locally on your device using encrypted MMKV storage
  • Data is backed up to iCloud if you have iCloud Backup enabled (Apple's standard backup, not controlled by us)
  • We cannot access your local data - it never leaves your device

3.2 Premium Users (Cloud Storage)

  • Data stored in Supabase (secure PostgreSQL database hosted on AWS)
  • Data encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Data encrypted at rest (database-level encryption)
  • Access controlled via anonymous authentication (no passwords stored)
  • Only your invited household members can access your shared data

4. Your Data Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

4.1 Access

  • Free users: All your data is on your device. Go to Settings > History to view it.
  • Premium users: Contact us at privacy@bryan.wtf to request a copy of your cloud data.

4.2 Deletion

  • Free users: Delete individual feeding logs, pets, or all data via Settings > "Delete All Data"
  • Premium users:
    • Leave household: Settings > Members > "Leave Household" (deletes your cloud data)
    • Delete account: Contact privacy@bryan.wtf

4.3 Data Portability

  • Free users: Data is stored in JSON format on your device. We can provide export instructions upon request.
  • Premium users: Contact privacy@bryan.wtf to receive your data in a portable format (JSON or CSV).

5. Third-Party Services

We use these trusted service providers to operate the app:

Service Purpose Data Shared

RevenueCat Subscription management Anonymous user ID, purchase history
Apple Push Notification Service Feeding reminders Device token (no personal data)

6. Children's Privacy

PetCrew is not directed to children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at privacy@bryan.wtf and we will delete it.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted:

  • In the app (Settings > Privacy Policy)
  • On our website (bryan.wtf/petcrew/privacy)

Material changes (e.g., new data collection) will require your consent before taking effect.

8. Contact Us

If you have questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or your data:

  • Email: privacy@bryan.wtf
  • Support: bryan@bryan.wtf
  • Website: https://bryan.wtf/petcrew

Response Time: We aim to respond within 48 hours for privacy inquiries.
